How Does This Work?

Passion Pods are small, community-driven groups where teammates come together around a shared interest or activity.

1. Create or join a pod

  • Browse the Passion Pods board to see what's been proposed.
  • You can join an existing pod or propose a new one.
  • A pod is only activated once it has at least one +1 (no solo pods — this is about building community!).

2. Pod creators: confirm +1 and set details

If you propose a pod:

  • Check back on the board to confirm someone has joined your pod.
  • You cannot move forward until another teammate has joined.
  • Once confirmed, update your pod with the date and time as soon as it's established.

3. Use your morale spend

  • Your per-person morale stipend is what you use to fund or participate in your pod activity.
  • Pod activities must follow standard morale spend guidelines.
  • To keep things simple and fair, please join only ONE pod.

4. Do the activity & share

  • Pods complete their activity locally (same kit, ingredients, or theme).
  • Photos from pod activities will be shared during team syncs, so everyone can celebrate and learn from each other.

Reminder: Choose activities that create a shared experience and story — not just a purchase.

Pod Examples

Below are examples of Passion Pods to help spark ideas. These are just starting points — pods can be adapted based on local availability.

Bake-Along Pod
Everyone bakes the same cake or cookie recipe using locally sourced ingredients.
Bread Basics Pod
A shared focus on making bread (same technique, flexible recipes).
Science at Home Pod
Simple, safe science experiment kits or guided experiments done locally.
Paint & Create Pod
Paint-by-number, watercolor, or simple art kits — same medium, individual styles.
DIY Craft Pod
Clay, origami, embroidery, or other hands-on crafts sourced locally.
Terrarium Builders Pod
Build small plant terrariums using materials from local shops.
Book Club Pod
Everyone reads the same book or topic and shares takeaways.
Learn Something New Pod
A short course or learning kit, followed by a quick share-back.
Regional Recipe Pod
Same base recipe, with local or cultural twists shared afterward.
Mini Maker Pod
Small build projects (e.g., beginner kits or household builds) completed and shared.

Reminder: Pods require at least one +1 to activate — check the board and join what excites you!
