Create a Pod

🚀

Thank you for creating a pod!

Your contribution helps build our community and keeps the Activity Pod initiative thriving. Here's what to expect:

  • Follow morale spending policy — Make sure your activity is compliant. Pods should not involve purchasing anything dangerous or consist solely of purchasing alcoholic beverages, among other policy guidelines.
  • Admin review first — Your pod will be sent to Antonio for approval and will not appear on the Pod Proposals page yet. Expect a response within 24–48 hours. You'll be notified whether your pod was approved or, if rejected, given the reason why.
  • Voting phase — Once approved, your pod moves to the Pod Proposals page where coworkers can vote for it. Your pod needs 3 votes to become active — voting also means they're joining the pod!
  • Morale budget available only when active — You cannot proceed with your pod or use morale money until it becomes active and is visible on the main Activity Pods dashboard.
  • No notification system yet — As the pod owner, it's your responsibility to keep tabs on your pod's progress — from gathering enough votes to become active, to seeing who joins. Check back regularly!

About This Project

Care Activity Pods is a community-driven platform that helps teams organize and join group activities — called "pods." Whether it's a hiking trip, a cooking class, or a game night, pods make it easy to find people who share your interests.

Built with ❤️ using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Firebase. The space theme with floating rocket cats represents the limitless possibilities when people come together!

Created by: Antonio Alejandro

Purpose: Helping teams build connections through shared activities
